| `employee_deduction_amount`                     | *Optional[str]*                                 | :heavy_minus_sign:                              | Employee deduction amount towards the benefit   |
| `benefit_id`                                    | *Optional[int]*                                 | :heavy_minus_sign:                              | The ID of the benefit.                          |
